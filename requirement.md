Business Specs
The Enterprise Knowledge Assistant platform must consolidate information from multiple sources (SharePoint, Confluence, local servers, databases) and make it accessible through a unified interface. It should understand natural language queries and provide contextually relevant answers based on the company's internal knowledge.
The platform should support personalized experiences based on the employee's role, department, and access permissions. It must maintain security and compliance with data governance policies while providing relevant information to authorized users. The assistant should help employees find documents, answer procedural questions, summarize lengthy content, and connect employees with subject matter experts when needed.
Key capabilities include:
Natural language query processing across all knowledge sources
Role-based access control and personalized responses
Document discovery and summarization
Process guidance for common workflows
Expert identification and connection
Learning from user interactions to improve over time
